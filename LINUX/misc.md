## **Index-Based Circuit Breaker System (NEPSE)**

|**Trigger Level**|**Trigger Event**|**Threshold**|**Penalty / Action**|
|---|---|---|---|
|First Level|Index movement within early trading|5% up or down (within first 2 hours)|Trading halted for 15 minutes|
|Second Level|Further index movement|Additional 8% move (any time after resumption)|Market closed for the rest of the day|

---

## **Summary of the New Circuit Breaker Rules**

|**Feature**|**New Regulatory Provision (Effective April 8)**|
|---|---|
|Individual Stock Price Limit|Daily price fluctuation limit increased from **10% to 15%**|
|Pre‑Open Session Limit|Price movement limit increased from **2% to 3%**|
|First Index Halt (5%)|Trading paused for **15 minutes** if the index moves **±5% within the first 2 hours**|
|Full Market Closure (8%)|Trading closed for the **entire day** if the index moves an additional **±8%**|
|Number of Index Halts|Maximum **two circuit breaker triggers per trading day**|
|After‑Market Orders|Orders allowed between **6:00 PM and 6:00 AM**|

## **Individual Stock Circuit Breaker (At a Glance)**

|**Item**|**Old Rule**|**New Rule**|
|---|---|---|
|Daily Stock Circuit Limit|±10%|**±15%**|
|Applicable Session|Continuous & Pre‑open|Continuous & Pre‑open (with revised limits)|
